    Demand Dimensions¶

    The 12 Pillars of Demand Classification¶

    The core of the taxonomy is built upon 12 independent dimensions. Each dimension captures a unique aspect of the demand signal, allowing for a precise "SKU Fingerprint" to be generated.

    📂 Explore Dimensions¶

    # Dimension Focus
    1 Lifecycle Product maturity and commercial stage.
    2 Behavior Statistical patterns of volume and frequency (ADI/CV²).
    3 Driver The underlying cause of demand movement.
    4 Magnitude Absolute volume and value tiering.
    5 Trend Long-term directional movement.
    6 Concentration Density and peakedness of demand.
    7 Shock Resilience and recovery from disruptions.
    8 Elasticity Sensitivity to price and promotional stimulus.
    9 Timing Lead/Lag and arrival characteristics.
    10 Recurrence Consistency of repeat purchase intervals.
    11 Interaction Cross-SKU relationships (Halo/Cannibalism).
    12 Signal Noise levels and data quality metrics.

    📋 Using the Dimensions¶

    Every SKU in your portfolio should be assessed against all 12 dimensions. The result is a 12-Dimension SKU Fingerprint that determines the optimal forecasting model and business strategy.
